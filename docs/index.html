About Me

I'm a fairly laid back otter. As you can see in my photo, I'm a big fan of the "outdoorsy" look. My best times in life are when I'm floating in the sea, eating oysters off of my sizable belly. I really enjoy the freedom of floating amongst the mist on an early morning in the bay.

Other than that, I love coming home and coding on my trusty MacBook Pro while sipping coffee.
